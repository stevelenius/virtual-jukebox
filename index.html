Virtual Jukebox: 1939 Seeburg Symphonola Vogue

  1. Demonstration version: 15-second song samples
  2. Click the red selectors to select the songs you want to hear
  3. Click the red selector to deselect a song if you change your mind
  4. After selecting, click to insert quarter, dime or nickel
  5. Song waveform shows at bottom of screen as it is playing
